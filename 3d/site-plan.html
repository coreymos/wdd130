Overview

Purpose

I’m a computer programmer. I make websites and programs. I also make 3D Modeled art. My website provides a catalog of 3d models and information about where to buy or download them. Making 3D models takes a lot of time. Businesses and hobbyists can save time by using other people’s models in their projects. My website will be a way for artists to see examples of 3d art and information regarding those pieces of art.

Audience

I am targeting an audience of people who are digital 3D artists or people or like to view art. People who are making their own art and want to find out where to download or buy the models on the site will have access to do that. These are people who are most likely on a desktop or laptop. When it comes to the demographic, I would assume it would be mostly younger people but not limited to younger people.

Content

Home page

As technology continues to advance, the accessibility to different mediums of art continues to also increase. Digital three-dimensional art is no exception to this. Blender, a free open-source program which can be used to manipulate a three-dimensional space, is gaining popularity in recent years due to its accessibility of being free, and also its large catalog of free learning educational tools. All of these things combined have allowed artists to express themselves in a new category, digital 3D. The purpose of this website is to further increase accessibility by allowing artists to easily find a catalog of other artist’s work. One of the cool features of digital art is that you can recycle other artist’s work into your own to make your own creative work. Here you can look at and admire other artist’s work from which I have handpicked. These pieces of art include links to which you can download the file containing the art pictured. 3D models are used for all sorts of things in today’s world. These range from specific dimensioned mechanical parts, to 3D printable objects, or even objects used in a video game. Look around at the models and explore the possibilities of which you can use them for your own purpose. There are countless things you can do with a 3D model so if you find something that catches your eye and sparks a lightbulb in your head, don’t hesitate to download that model and manifest that idea into reality.

Educational Information

Where can you learn how to 3D model? I would recommend the software Blender as it is free. As a result of this, a lot of people use the software and make tutorials on youtube for the software. There is a plethora of free educational content all over the internet for this software so in my opinion, it is the best place to start developing skills in 3D modeling. In Blender, you can animate or make images. The only stipulation is that you need a PC that is able to handle computing and calculating all of the different things within the software. For example, there is a lighting system within the software that simulates light in real life. This simulation is intense of the PC and as long as you have a system that can handle that, you are all set to start your journey in 3D modeling. How do you download the content being shown on this website? Well if it is an art image, all you have to do is right click the image and save it. If it is a 3D model, there is a link provided to the website that contains the 3d model and all of its different types of downloadable files. These different file types depend on what program you are using to open the file and manipulate it. The model can be used in a game making software, an animation software, a 3d printing software, etc. So it is important to know your purpose of the model beforehand, in order to know the right file type to download.
